What You Do

Share what you already know

Foreigners are fascinated by Kenya — its language, food, culture, and people. You're the expert. They pay to learn from you.

🗣️

Teach Basic Kiswahili

Greetings, numbers, common phrases — help foreigners learn the language they've always been curious about.

"Karibu sana!" — Very welcome!
🍛

Kenyan Food & Culture

Tell them about ugali, nyama choma, matatu culture — the real Kenya that tourists never see.

"Chakula ni tamu" — Food is delicious
🌍

African History & Geography

Share knowledge about East Africa, Kenyan history, and famous landmarks. Foreigners love learning this.

"Afrika ni nzuri" — Africa is beautiful
😄

Casual Friendly Chat

Sometimes they just want to talk to a real Kenyan. Make friends, share stories, and get paid for it.

"Maisha ni mazuri" — Life is good
💡

Local Tips & Advice

Help foreigners planning to visit Kenya. Best areas, safety tips, hidden gems — your local knowledge is gold.

"Nairobi inapendeza" — Nairobi is lovely
🎵

Music & Slang

Sheng, bongo flava, Afrobeats — teach them what's hot in Kenya right now. They're obsessed with it.

"Muziki ni roho" — Music is the soul
